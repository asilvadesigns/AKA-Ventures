About Us

AKA Ventures is a Washington, DC venture capital firm that supports the most promising emerging technology-enabled companies, helping them become global brands beloved by their customers.

Our team is like our entrepreneurs - We move fast, think big, and are committed to building the next major franchises in technology.

Founded by Aaron Drew and Michael Kapoor, the firm provides entrepreneurs with access to expertise and insights in innovation, executive and technical talent, market intelligence, policy and regulatory affairs, business development and marketing & brand-building.
